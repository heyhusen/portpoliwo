## [3.0.0] - 2022-02-09

### Added

- Improved testing environment

### Changed

- Replace yarn with pnpm
- Replace Laravel Mix with vite
- Upgrade Vue from v2 to v3
- Replace Buefy with Headless UI, Oruga UI, and WindiCSS

- Vue auto routing
